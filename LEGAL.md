# Legal & Ethical Use

This app performs active security testing: network scanning, credential
brute-forcing, and SQL injection probing. These are real, working tools —
not simulations — and using them against a system generates real traffic and
real load on that system.

**Only point these tools at:**

- Devices and networks you personally own (your home Wi-Fi, your own
  router, your own phone/laptop).
- Web applications, login forms, and databases you built and control, or
  that you hold **explicit, documented, written** authorization to test
  (e.g. a signed pentest engagement letter or bug bounty program scope).
- CTF or training targets that are designed to be tested (HackTheBox,
  TryHackMe, DVWA, and similar).

**Do not** point these tools at systems you do not own and do not have
written authorization to test — including your employer's production
systems without a signed engagement, third-party services, or anyone else's
accounts or infrastructure. Unauthorized access or unauthorized impairment
of computer systems is a criminal offense in most jurisdictions (for
example, the U.S. Computer Fraud and Abuse Act, the UK Computer Misuse Act,
and equivalent laws elsewhere), independent of intent.

Using this app means you take full responsibility for confirming
authorization for every target before you scan it.
